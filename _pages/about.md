Dr. Rahul Sharma is an academic leader, researcher, and institutional strategist with over 18 years of experience in higher education across Europe and India. Currently a Senior Researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany, he contributes to large-scale European initiatives in artificial intelligence, intelligent data systems, and digital innovation.

He has held key academic leadership roles in India, including Professor, Head of Department, and Dean of Innovation and International Affairs, where he led major institutional transformation initiatives. His leadership resulted in the establishment of interdisciplinary research ecosystems and innovation platforms, including the IDEA Lab, enabling over 450 patent filings and strengthening industry collaboration, startup incubation, and technology commercialization.

Dr. Sharma brings a unique blend of academic and industry experience, with expertise in digital transformation, data-driven governance, and AI-enabled systems. He has contributed to research projects exceeding €15 million in funding and maintains strong international collaborations across Europe and Asia.

His work is driven by a vision to build globally competitive, research-intensive, and future-ready universities, with a focus on innovation, governance, and societal impact.


Dr. Sharma’s leadership philosophy emphasizes the integration of research excellence, digital transformation, global engagement, and sustainable institutional growth. He is particularly committed to aligning university education with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), promoting responsible innovation, ethical AI, and technology-driven solutions to global challenges.

Through his work, Dr. Sharma continues to contribute to the development of globally competitive, innovation-driven academic institutions that combine academic rigor, technological advancement, and societal impact.
